We’re Weston Estate—a group of five friends who started making music in our bedrooms and never stopped. Blending elements of R&B, indie, and alternative sounds, we create songs that reflect the highs, lows, and everything in between. Our name comes from the suburban neighborhood where we grew up, but our sound goes way beyond that. For us, music is a way to stay connected to each other and to you. Whether you’ve been with us since the beginning or just found your way here, thanks for listening. Welcome to the family.
